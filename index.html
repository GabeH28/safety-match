About

Safety Match is a New York-based electro-acoustic duo founded by clarinetist Gabe Henkin and pianist Olivia Qing Zhao. Classically trained at the Manhattan School of Music, the two fuse instrumental virtuosity with modern technology, looping and processing their instruments into new, vibrant forms.

Drawing from jazz fusion, pop, hip-hop, classical, and world music, their sound is unbound by genre. Blending their diverse musical heritages, Safety Match creates concerts that are as much visual as sonic, incorporating audio-reactive projections that respond in real time to their music.

Their debut EP, Electric Impulses, is out now on their independent label, Diffuser.
